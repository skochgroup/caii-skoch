Madan Mohan

Madan Mohan

Madan Mohan is a post graduate from IARI and had his management education from IIM Ahmedabad and Asian Institute of Management, Manila. As development management professional, he did pioneer work in developing SHGs in the state of J&K, skilling and financing of rural enterprises in Punjab. He has been to Harvard for advance course in appraisal of agri projects. Formerly, CGM, NABARD and CEO of NABARD Consulting, he consults with ministry of Agriculture and is a faculty with National Institute of Banking studies and Corporate Management (NIBSCOM).

Updesh Kumar Sharma

Updesh Kumar Sharma

Updesh Kumar Sharma is a former Adviser (Infrastructure and S&T) Planning Commission and NITI Aayog. He is an Electrical Engineer, MBA and MA (Public Policy & Sustainable Development). He has played a key role in setting up Atal Innovation Mission at NITI Aayog for radically transforming innovations and entrepreneurship eco-system in the country. He has also worked with NHPC and BHEL in various capacities and been on the Board of Central PSUs. He has participated in various academic and training programmes at leading Universities of US, Australia, South Korea.

Bhushan Mohan

Bhushan Mohan

Bhushan Mohan is an industry veteran and worked as Principal Consultant, NeGD, MeitY, Govt of India. He was responsible for formulation of policies, guidelines and strategy for implementation of NeGP with a vision to bring public services within reach of every citizen. He has contributed extensively in areas of policy formulation, strategic planning, procurement and technology assessment. He is an independent Consultant and a Mentor and also works as Advisor to PWC.

Previously, Neelam was the Managing Director of Microsoft India, a position she held from 2005 to 2008. During her tenure, she sharpened Microsoft’s strategic focus and improved its operating efficiency and execution, as well as its financial performance and customer focus.

Nirmal Bansal

Nirmal Bansal

Nirmal Bansal is a Certified Corporate Director-IOD/ MCA/IICA, M.IOD, Post graduate in commerce, CA IIB, PGDPR and Senior Fellow, SKOCH Development Foundation for over a decade. Surinder Singh Sur

Surinder Singh Sur

Surinder Singh Sur, Former Adviser, NITI Aayog is an Electrical Engineer by profession. He is a Master of Electrical Engineering from Delhi Technological University, MBA from University of Hull, UK and Master in Public Policy from TERI University. He was India’s representative at Development Policies programme in International Development Centre, Tokyo, Japan. He put together the blueprint for Integrated Energy Policy, 2008; represented India at G20 in China, Germany and Argentina. Now, he is an emeritus faculty in Pusa and Aryabhatt Institute of Technolgy, Delhi.
